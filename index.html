Hotspot App Privacy Policy

Welcome to the Hotspot app's privacy policy page. This page outlines the information we collect and how it's used when you use our app's features.

The Hotspot app collects certain information to provide you with a personalized and smooth experience.

Security

We prioritize the security of your data and take appropriate measures to protect it from unauthorized access or disclosure.

We also use industry-standard encryption techniques to protect your data in transit and at rest.
